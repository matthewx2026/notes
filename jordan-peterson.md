### 3. Beyond Order: 12 More Rules for Life (2021)
The sequel to 12 Rules for Life, offering additional guidance on finding meaning and structure in an increasingly chaotic world.

ðŸ“– [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/Beyond-Order-More-Rules-Life/dp/0593084640)

**The 12 Additional Rules:**
1. Do not carelessly denigrate social institutions or creative achievement
2. Imagine who you could be, and then aim single-mindedly at that
3. Do not hide unwanted things in the fog
4. Notice that opportunity lurks where responsibility has been abdicated
5. Do not do what you hate
6. Abandon ideology
7. Work as hard as you possibly can on at least one thing and see what happens
8. Try to make one room in your home as beautiful as possible
9. If old memories still upset you, write them down carefully and completely
10. Plan and work diligently to maintain the romance in your relationship
11. Do not allow yourself to become resentful, deceitful, or arrogant
12. Be grateful in spite of your suffering

## Key Themes for Parents of Teens

### From Peterson's teachings:

**On Discipline:**
> "You cannot be friends with your children. You need to be an authority figure first."

**On Encouragement:**
> "Encourage your children to take on difficult things, even knowing they might fail."

**On Smartphones (from Jonathan Haidt discussion):**
- Delaying smartphone access until age 14-16
- No social media before 16
- Phone-free schools
- More unsupervised outdoor play

**On Education:**
- Critical thinking over ideology
- Practical skills alongside academics
- Responsibility as a path to meaning
- Value of challenging children appropriately
